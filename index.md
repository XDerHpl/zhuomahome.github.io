# Zhuo Ma

<strong>Education and Professional Experience:</strong><br>
• Received B.Eng. (2003), M.Eng. (2006), and Ph.D. (2010) degrees in Computer Science and Technology / Computer Architecture from Xidian University.<br>
• 2010-2015: Lecturer / Associate Professor, School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University.<br>
• 2015-Present: Associate Professor / Professor, School of Cyber Engineering, Xidian University.<br>
• 2017-2018: Fully funded by China Scholarship Council as a visiting scholar at the University of Utah, advised by Prof. Feifei Li.

<strong>Research Area:</strong> privacy-preserving machine learning, adversarial/backdoor defense, federated learning security, and secure data sharing/computation.
