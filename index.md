# Zhuo Ma

<strong>Zhuo Ma (马卓)</strong> (<a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=uU5Q0mMAAAAJ">Google Scholar</a>) received his B.Eng. (2003), M.Eng. (2006), and Ph.D. (2010) degrees in Computer Science and Technology / Computer Architecture from Xidian University. He visited the University of Utah from 2017 to 2018 as a visiting scholar, advised by Prof. Feifei Li. Now he is a professor in School of Cyber Engineering, Xidian University.

<strong>Research Area:</strong> He is mainly engaged in research on artificial intelligence, unmanned systems security, privacy-preserving computation, and large language model security.

<strong>实验室长期招收本校及外校优秀本科生进行科研实习。同时常年招聘博士后研究人员。每年均有硕士与博士研究生名额，欢迎联系申请！</strong>

<strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:mazhuo@mail.xidian.edu.cn">mazhuo@mail.xidian.edu.cn</a>
